WHAT IS USER ONBOARDING?

USER ONBOARDING IS THE PROCESS OF LEADING YOUR USERS TO BECOME SUCCESSFUL WITH YOUR PRODUCT

Internet is the new storefront. Customers search, buy and use your product online. User onboarding is about creating a desirable experience for new users to quickly introduce value of your product, eliminate confusion and drive engagement.

ABOUT ME

Roman Zadyrako
ONBOARDING PRO

I learned the importance of user onboarding by dealing with poor visitor-to-user conversions and lack of engagement with the product at three of my startup companies.

In 2013 user onboarding became my sole focus as I’ve seen how most small and medium companies overlook user onboarding, which is an essential tool for customer acquisition and growth.

I co-founded myTips – step-by-step tutorials without coding, and am currently working on Tooltip.io to create an onboarding toolbox to help product and marketing managers work engage and lead their users to success.
